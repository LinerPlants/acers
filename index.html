Growers and producers of grafted cultivars of Japanese Maple trees
 
            Acer palmatum
Acer dissectum
Acer shirasawanum
 
Trees are grafted to grow into a range of structures, from shrubs to high worked standards. All trees are provided bare rooted washed and packed in moist media then packed in cartons for airfreighting.
 
Freighted from New Zealand in June to August of each year,
with Phytosanitary Certification and ready for immediate planting.
